  • Keiths Famous Quiche Recipe

    So Baked

    Without further ado, here is the secret family recipe to my quiche: Ingredients 1 Pillsbury™ refrigerated pie crust, softened as directed on box 1 cup half-and-half or milk 4 eggs, slightly beaten 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon pepper 8 slices bacon, crisply cooked, crumbled 1cup shredded Swiss or Cheddar cheese (4 oz) 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese 1 tablespoon chopped onion, if desired Steps 1 Heat oven to 350°F. Place pie crust in 9-inch glass pie plate as directed on box for One-Crust Filled Pie. 2 In medium bowl, mix half-and-half, eggs, salt and pepper; set aside. Layer bacon, cheeses and onion in crust-lined plate. Pour egg mixture over top. 3 Bake 40 to 50 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Let stand 5 minutes; cut into wedges.
